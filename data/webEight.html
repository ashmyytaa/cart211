Be mindful of what you let out on the internet.

Surveillance capitalism is one of the top practices nowaydays as we shift alot of our daily pratices and routines on the internet and social media. Large corporations make huge profits by selling and collecting our data, to them use them towards us in the means of compelling us to use their platforms, interact with them, as well consume their products.

Many of the data we give out is by simply clicking the terms and conditions when it comes to download an app, or use a platform. We all blindlt accept the terms and conditions, usually because we dont want to read all the information that discloses the terms and conditions. Alot of crucial information is stated in these terms as it tells in which way a corporation is using our data and identity.

We should pay closer attention to what we accept and give to the internet, as we are getting used for the sole purpose of profitting these-scale corporations
